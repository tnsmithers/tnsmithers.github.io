My Job

Brand Communications Student Assistant

Currently, I have what I consider to be the best internship at UW student could have. I work at Wisconsin Athletics as a Brand Communication Student Assistant. That's a fancy was of saying I help run social media and do wrting for the athletics website (Visit uwbadgers.com). I am specifically assigned women's hockey, and men's and women's golf, meaning those sports are my responsibility in addition to my supervisors who also "own" that sport. My favorite is women's hockey. They are historically successful and have had a really good season so far. My task for women's hockey has been the Tik Tok page and we have already gained over 1,000 followers since I started posting. I love my job!
